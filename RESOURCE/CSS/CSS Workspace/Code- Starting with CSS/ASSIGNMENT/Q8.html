About Us

PW Skills is an online learning platform that offers courses on a variety of topics, including coding, data science, and digital marketing. The platform was founded in 2017 by Alakh Pandey, who is also the founder of Physics Wallah, a popular educational YouTube channel. PW Skills has over 7 million registered users and has helped over 100,000 students find jobs in the tech industry.

PW Skills offers a variety of features that make it a popular choice for online learners. These features include:

High-quality courses
PW Skills courses are taught by experienced instructors and cover a wide range of topics.

Flexible learning
PW Skills courses can be accessed on any device, at any time. This makes it easy to fit learning into your busy schedule.

Affordable pricing
PW Skills courses are priced affordably, making them accessible to a wide range of students.

If you are looking to learn new skills or advance your career in the tech industry, PW Skills is a great option.
