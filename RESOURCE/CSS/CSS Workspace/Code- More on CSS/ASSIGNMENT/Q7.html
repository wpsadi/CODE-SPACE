Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source typefaces that you can use on your website. It offers a wide variety of fonts, from classic serifs to modern sans serifs, and everything in between. You can browse the library by category, style, or popularity. Once you've found a font you like, you can simply copy and paste the embed code into your website.

OSWALD

Oswald is a modern sans serif typeface designed by Vernon Adams. It was first released in 2011 and is available for free under the SIL Open Font License. Oswald is a versatile font that can be used for a variety of purposes, including headlines, body text, and logos. It is characterized by its strong geometric forms, sharp edges, and wide slabs. Oswald is a popular choice for web design and is often used in conjunction with other modern sans serif fonts, such as Roboto and Lato.
